== Description ==

### MeteoSales: The Swiss Army knife solution for woocommerce

Every day there is a factor that is changing.This change is conditioning our buying behavior several times a day whether we know it or not and can count for up 40% of sales.This factor is the weather. 

We all know that putting the right product in front of a customers eyes is crucial for converting sales. We also know how our tastes and preferences can change with the seasons, well this can happen on a daily basis too and it’s not just about trends & style.

### WHAT METEOSALES DOES:

Dynamic weather-based product recommendation.Unlike trends that go away after some time, the influence of weather on consumer behavior is constant.

* Associate products,banners and CTA to specific weather conditions
* Automatic A/B test
* Dynamic product offering based on local user weather
* Powered by AI and machine learning

AI Content Writing.
Let our AI writing assistant do the heavy lifting for you?

* Access to all 10+ writing tools (and counting)
* Write memorable product descriptions
* Write sales & marketing emails that sell
* Generate catchy captions for social media
* Extract the right keywords from your texts
* Support for 50+ languages

Data Dashboard & Analytics.
Our data dashboard is a great way to get a high-level view of your analytics data.

* Most sold products

* Real-time Sales
* Real-Time Analytics
* UTM - medium
* In-depth user details
* Track page movement
* GDPR Compliant

Heatmaps.

Replays.

== Installation ==

Starting with MeteoSales consists of just two steps: installing and setting up the plugin.

### INSTALL METEOSALES MANUALLY 

1. Upload `meteosales.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Go to ‘after activation’ below.
